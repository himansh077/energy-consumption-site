Powerful Energy Management Features

Discover how PowerTrack helps you monitor, analyze, and optimize your electricity consumption with our comprehensive feature set.

Core Features

Real-time Monitoring

Track your electricity usage with minute-by-minute updates through our intuitive dashboard.

Usage Analytics

Detailed breakdowns of your consumption patterns by device, time of day, and cost.

Smart Alerts

Get notified when usage exceeds thresholds or when energy rates change.

Cost Tracking

See exactly how much each appliance costs to run and identify savings opportunities.

Mobile Access

Full control and monitoring from our iOS and Android apps.

Historical Data

Compare current usage with past months and years to track your progress.

AI-Powered Recommendations

Our intelligent system analyzes your usage patterns and provides personalized recommendations to reduce consumption and save money. The more you use PowerTrack, the smarter it gets at identifying savings opportunities specific to your home.

  • Optimal appliance scheduling
  • Peak hour avoidance
  • Equipment upgrade suggestions
  • Behavioral recommendations

Comprehensive Reporting

Generate detailed reports for any time period with visualizations that make your energy data easy to understand. Export reports as PDF or CSV for sharing with family members or energy consultants.

Key report types include:

  • Monthly consumption trends
  • Appliance-specific usage
  • Cost projections
  • Savings achievement
  • Carbon footprint analysis
